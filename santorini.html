Santorini

Art 127–Graphic Symbolism

For this project, my tasked is to create a logo for a city of my choice but end up with an island that I will one day visit, Santorini. The island is known for its view and stunning sunset and unique architecture. I created the logo that represents the three bells of Fira that is located on top of the Greek Catholic Church, which is known for its rounded bright blue roof and white bright walls.

Service: Logo Design
Skills: Sketching, Adobe Illustrator
