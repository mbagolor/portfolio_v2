Stamp

ART 125-Intro to Graphic Design

In this task, I collaborated with two of my classmates on creating a stamp. The theme we decided on were crystals. I work on painting the crystals and scanned them into the computer. The next person made lines and defined the crystals through editing and refining. Lastly, the third person decided to input all the images together and incorporate the best typeface that best fits.

Service: Graphic Design
Skills: Sketching, Painting, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
