Rotoscoping

Simple Animation Design

about this project

This project was a fun digital imaging that was created in photoshop and later on transfered into After Effect to create a small and fun video. The digital imagine was a rotoscope using short video clips. The objective was to create a theme and keeping a cohesive aesthetic among all three moving images. The theme to my rotoscope was simplicity, 2-3 colors and simple lines. Above this page contain a video that explains why I chose these short video clips to rotoscope.
